Banana Splits

Description

A banana split is a classic, indulgent dessert that’s perfect for satisfying your sweet tooth. It features a split banana as the base, topped with three scoops of ice cream—traditionally vanilla, chocolate, and strawberry. Each scoop is then drizzled with different sauces like chocolate syrup, pineapple, and strawberry sauce. To finish, the banana split is garnished with whipped cream, chopped nuts, and a cherry on top. This dessert is a fun and colorful treat that can be customized with various toppings to suit your taste.

Ingredients

  • 1 ripe banana
  • 1 scoop vanilla ice cream
  • 1 scoop chocolate ice cream
  • 1 scoop strawberry ice cream
  • 2 tbsp chocolate syrup
  • 2 tbsp pineapple topping
  • 2 tbsp strawberry sauce
  • Whipped cream
  • Chopped nuts (optional)
  • Maraschino cherry

Directions

  1. Peel the banana and slice it lengthwise in half. Place the banana halves in a dish with the cut sides facing up.
  2. Place a scoop of vanilla ice cream, chocolate ice cream, and strawberry ice cream in a row between the banana halves.
  3. Drizzle the vanilla ice cream with pineapple topping, the chocolate ice cream with chocolate syrup, and the strawberry ice cream with strawberry sauce.
  4. Top each scoop of ice cream with a dollop of whipped cream.
  5. Sprinkle with chopped nuts, if desired.
  6. Add a maraschino cherry on top of the whipped cream for the finishing touch.
