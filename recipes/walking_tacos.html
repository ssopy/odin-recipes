Walking Tacos

Description

A walking taco is a fun, portable twist on the traditional taco, perfect for casual get-togethers, outdoor events, or even a quick family meal. Instead of serving the taco fillings in a tortilla, everything is piled into a small bag of chips—typically Fritos or Doritos—allowing you to “walk” while eating it. The bag is filled with taco-seasoned ground beef, cheese, lettuce, salsa, and any other toppings you desire. This easy-to-make recipe is not only customizable but also a crowd-pleaser.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 packet taco seasoning
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 8 small bags of Fritos or Doritos
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1/2 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup sour cream
  • 1/4 cup salsa

    • Optional Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup sliced jalapeños
  • 1/4 cup black olives

Directions

  1. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef over medium heat until fully browned, breaking it apart as it cooks.
  2. Drain any excess grease, then stir in the taco seasoning and water. Simmer for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the beef is well-coated in the seasoning.
  3. Open each bag of Fritos or Doritos, gently crushing the chips inside to break them into smaller pieces.
  4. Spoon a generous portion of seasoned ground beef into each bag of chips.
  5. Top with shredded cheddar cheese, lettuce, tomatoes, sour cream, salsa, and any other desired toppings like jalapeños or black olives.
  6. Serve with a fork, allowing everyone to enjoy their “walking taco” directly from the chip bag.
